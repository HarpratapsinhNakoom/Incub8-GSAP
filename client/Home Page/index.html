ABOUT US
Incub8 is a section-8, non-profit organization with an aim to work in line with the national priorities and goals and its focus would be on nurturing ideas and innovations into successful startups to build an innovation driven entrepreneurial ecosystem with an objective of socio-economic development through wealth and job creation.

With your guidance and support, we intend to ensure Incub8 becomes a state-of-the-art world class incubation facility with suitable physical infrastructure in terms of capital equipment and operating facilities, coupled with the availability of sectoral experts for mentoring, business planning support, access to seed capital, industry partners, trainings and other relevant components to become scalable and sustainable enterprises.
SUPPORT FOR STARTUPS
Incubation program
Incubation programs offer a comprehensive package of resources and support, including mentorship, office space, networking opportunities, and access to funding, to help startups develop and scale their businesses.
Co-working environment
Co-working environments provide shared office space for individuals and companies, fostering collaboration, community, and cost-efficiency through flexible and customizable membership options.
Mentoring support
Mentoring support offers guidance and advice from experienced professionals to help individuals and organizations develop skills, overcome challenges, and achieve their personal and professional goals.
Network
Access to a strong network across government departments, corporate organizations and a pool of investors.
